## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

1. reviewing and merging pull requests
2. triaging issues
3. participating in ADR decisions
4. mentoring contributors
5. preserving quality, security, and release integrity

See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) for the maintainer process.
